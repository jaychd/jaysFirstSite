About Me

I was born and raised in San Antonio, Texas and graduated from St. Mary's University in 2011 while working as a student for the Department of Defense. I later took a job with Apple and later moved to Austin, Texas in 2016 in pursuit of better opportunies within the company. It ended up not working out and a few months later I took a position with a Home Health company as a Help Desk Specialist and later promoted to their IT Manager.

After working there for 3 years, I took a position with the State of Texas working for the Texas Parks & Wildlife department. I was thankful for the opportunity but I ultimately chose to leave my position to focus on trying to find my place in the IT world.

Why I chose to be a Full-Stack Developer

Im a tech nerd who's worn many hats the past 10 years working in the IT industry. I've always classified myself as a person whos good in a lot of areas but never an expert in one. I grew somewhat tired of not having a specialty and earning a mediocre salary. I knew I was meant for something more. So after much debate I resigned from my position at Texas Parks & Wildlife and decided to pursue a career in web development. I hope that this leap of faith leads me to a long and fruitful career as a Full-Stack Developer!
